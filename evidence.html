SUBJECT: RECOVERY OF PERSONAL EFFECTS (CASE: CARLA OVIDI)
Location of Discovery

Sub-level 1, north corridor intersection (approximately 12 meters from the sealed entrance of Room 24).

Items were found undisturbed. No signs of struggle in the immediate recovery area. [FIELD NOTE REDACTED — SEE SUPPLEMENT B]

Recovered Items

One (1) leather backpack containing civilian clothing, personal keys, and an empty water bottle.

Condition: intact. No evidence of forced removal from subject.

One (1) paper map of the facility. Several corridors have been aggressively crossed out by the user with a red pen.

Notable: Room 24 is circled. A corridor connecting Sector B to [REDACTED] has been marked with the notation: "don't go back"

Alert: Magnetic Media

A series of standard VHS tapes have been located scattered across different rooms of the facility (Sector B). They do not belong to our security archives.

Preliminary analysis indicates these tapes contain raw footage of the facility's interior, tracking the exact chronological movement and route of the missing civilian. It appears her entire progression through the structure was dynamically recorded and distributed onto these cassettes by the facility's secondary terminal signals before her disappearance.

PLAYBACK AUTHORIZATION: Playback is authorized ONLY via isolated terminal screens. Standard monitors have exhibited distortion, signal bleed, and in one case — [FIELD TECH REPORT REDACTED]. Do not connect to networked systems.
